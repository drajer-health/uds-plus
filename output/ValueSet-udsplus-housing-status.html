ValueSet: Uds Plus Housing Status Codes (Experimental)

Official URL: http://fhir.org/guides/hrsa/uds-plus/ValueSet/udsplus-housing-status Version: 1.0.2
Standards status: Informative Computable Name: UdsPlusHousingStatusCodes

The Uds Plus Housing Status Codes is a ‘starter set’ of codes for identifying the Patient’s housing status.
