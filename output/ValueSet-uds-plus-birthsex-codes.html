ValueSet: Uds Plus Birth Sex Codes

Official URL: http://fhir.org/guides/hrsa/uds-plus/ValueSet/uds-plus-birthsex-codes Version: 1.0.2
Standards status: Trial-use Maturity Level: 2 Computable Name: UdsPlusBirthSexCodes

The Uds Plus Birth Sex Codes is a set of codes for identifying the birth sex of Patients.
